Who We Are

Empowering Women.
Building Community.

Sisters4Sisters Inc. is a 501(c)3 Non-Profit Organization recognized by the Internal Revenue Service, dedicated to connecting and celebrating women while driving meaningful change in our communities.

Through sisterhood, advocacy, and a deep commitment to service, we support women across all walks of life — lifting each other up through programs, events, and mutual support.

Our Programs & Initiatives

We run year-round programs that make a real difference in women's lives and our broader community.

National Black HIV/AIDS Awareness

Raising awareness, reducing stigma, and providing resources around HIV/AIDS in the Black community. Education and advocacy that saves lives.

The Prayer Room

"Prayer is the Answer." We create sacred spaces for spiritual reflection, healing, and collective prayer as a foundation for wellness.

Our holiday initiative ensures no child is left behind. Gift drives and adoption programs bringing joy to families in need each holiday season.
